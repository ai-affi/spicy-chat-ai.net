Yes, SpicyChat AI offers a free tier with basic access to community bots and limited daily messages. For unlimited messaging, priority queue, voice features, and mobile app access, you can upgrade to Premium.

Absolutely. SpicyChat AI is specifically designed for adult content and NSFW roleplay. Unlike Character AI which has strict SFW filters, SpicyChat has no content restrictions for adult scenarios. Users must be 18+.

How do I create a chatbot on SpicyChat?

Click 'Create' in the navigation menu, choose 'Chatbot', then fill in your character's name, personality traits, backstory, and upload an avatar. You can define greeting messages, example dialogs, and even connect lorebooks for complex world-building.

How does the voice feature work?

SpicyChat AI's voice feature uses AI-generated speech to bring your characters to life. Premium users can enable voice for any bot, choosing from different voice styles. The voice adapts to the character's personality for immersive roleplay.

What are lorebooks?

Lorebooks are SpicyChat's world-building system. They allow you to define complex settings, rules, relationships, and background information that your bot references during conversations. Perfect for intricate fantasy or sci-fi scenarios.

SpicyChat AI uses encryption for conversations and does not share your chats publicly. However, as with any online platform, avoid sharing personal information with AI characters. The platform is operated by NextDay AI Inc, a legitimate company based in Canada.

How do group chats work?

Group chats allow multiple AI characters to interact in the same conversation. You can create scenarios where 2, 3, or more bots talk to each other and to you. Perfect for complex roleplay with multiple personalities and relationship dynamics.
